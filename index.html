Key metrics

KPIsCLV&CAC

An ideal LTV:CAC ratio should be 3:1.The value of a customer should be three times more than the cost of acquiring them.
If the ratio is close i.e.1:1, you are spending too much.
If it’s 5:1, you are spending too little. In fact, you are probably missing out on business.
